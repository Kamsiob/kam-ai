# Kam AI Privacy Policy

**Effective date: [set on publication]**

Kam AI is built so that this policy can be short.

## The short version

Kam AI does not collect your data. Not "collects but anonymizes," not "collects but doesn't sell." It does not collect it. Everything you type, say, or store in Kam AI stays on your phone.

## What happens on your device

Conversations, memory, projects, follow-ups, voice recordings and their transcriptions, attached files, and settings are all processed and stored locally on your phone. None of it is transmitted to us or anyone else. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics, so there is nowhere for your data to go even if we wanted it to.

When Kam AI checks your phone's memory (RAM) to recommend a model size, that check happens on the device and the result never leaves it.

## When the app touches the internet

Kam AI uses the network only when you ask it to, for these things:

- **Downloading an AI model, voice, or content pack.** These are ordinary file downloads over HTTPS from public hosting (GitHub and, for some models, Hugging Face). Like any web request, the hosting server sees standard technical request data, such as your IP address, under that host's own privacy policy. The request contains nothing about you or your use of the app beyond the file being fetched.
- **Web search, if you set it up.** Search is off by default and only works if you connect your own search endpoint or API key. When you use it, your search queries go to the endpoint you chose, under that service's privacy policy. We never see them.
- **Links you tap.** Links in the About screen or on Discover cards open in your browser.

That is the complete list. If you never tap a download button and never set up search, Kam AI works entirely offline and makes no network requests.

## What Google sees

If you install Kam AI from Google Play, Google provides us aggregate statistics like install counts, as it does for every app. That comes from Google's systems, not from anything inside Kam AI, and is governed by Google's privacy policy.

## Backups

The backup file you can export contains your conversations, memory, and projects. It is created on your device and saved wherever you choose. It is yours; store it as carefully as you would anything private.

## Reporting a response

If you report an AI response as offensive or harmful, the app opens an email draft in your email app containing the response text. Nothing is sent unless you send it, and it goes to hello@kamsiob.com, read by one person.

## Children

Kam AI is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone, children included, because we do not collect information.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date, and the change will be noted in the app's release notes. Given how the app is built, meaningful changes are unlikely.
